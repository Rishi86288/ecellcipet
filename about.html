About E-Cell CIPET IPT Ahmedabad

Building a campus culture where entrepreneurship is a career path, not a side project.

Introduction

Who We Are

E-Cell CIPET IPT Ahmedabad is the official Entrepreneurship Cell of the Central Institute of Petrochemicals Engineering & Technology's Institute of Plastics Technology, Ahmedabad. Founded by a group of students who believed the campus needed more than technical training, it now runs the entrepreneurial ecosystem for the institute.

We connect students with mentors, industry professionals, and each other — through bootcamps, hackathons, pitch competitions, and a growing community of founders and builders.

Quick Facts

🏛️ Affiliated with CIPET IPT Ahmedabad

📅 Active since 2021

👥 1200+ members across all departments

🤝 Partnered with 15+ startup ecosystem organizations

🎯

Vision

To make CIPET IPT Ahmedabad a recognized launchpad for student-led ventures in the materials, manufacturing, and technology sectors.

🚀

Mission

To equip every student with the mindset, network, and hands-on experience needed to build something of their own — inside or outside the classroom.

📌

Objectives

Run 50+ high-quality events annually, mentor 30+ early-stage ideas, and build direct bridges between students and the startup ecosystem.
